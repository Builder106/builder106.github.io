Welcome to the portfolio. What you're looking at is fifteen projects, laid out like a 3D server room.

Every rack is a different build. We've got clusters for quant, software, analyst roles, security, and A.I. — and they're all color-coded.

If you just sit back for about fifteen seconds, you'll see a light wave sweep across the room, rack by rack.

Click one open, and you get the whole story right there. You'll see the read-me file, the repo activity, and a quick demo video.

Right in the middle is the central console. That's your control panel. You can use it to adjust the audio, check your session uptime, and view repo telemetry.

Want to get in touch? Just hit the ping button.

This entire experience was proudly built using React Three Fiber, Three dot J-S, and Blender
